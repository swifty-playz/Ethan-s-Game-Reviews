Borderlands 4

Borderlands 4 is coming to continue the Borderlands franchise by delivering a story-rich, semi-open-world, and action-packed adventure. Set on the newly unveiled planet Kairos, players will confront the tyrannical Timekeeper and his synthetic army, the Order, in a narrative that unfolds six years after the events of Borderlands 3. Be ready to explore a world with diverse environments, battle against enemies, and upgrade your character and weapons. The game supports cross-platform multiplayer, allowing up to four players to team up across different systems! So be excited to play with your friends on any platform! Borderlands 4 is set to release on September 12, 2025, for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC, with a Nintendo Switch 2 version to follow later in the year.

Persona5: The Phantom X

Persona 5: The Phantom X is a story-rich, turn-based RPG that blends social simulation with dungeon exploration in a stylized Tokyo. Players assume the role of a new Phantom Thief, balancing daily life activities like attending school and building relationships with nighttime missions in the Metaverse. The game introduces new characters and features, including rhythm-based mini-games and a PvE mode called Velvet Trials. While it incorporates gacha elements, it retains the essence of the Persona series. This free-to-play game is set to release globally on June 26, 2025, for PC and mobile platforms.
