Borderlands 4

Borderlands 4 is coming to continue the Borderlands franchise by delivering a story-rich, semi-open-world, and action-packed adventure. Set on the new planet, Kairos, players will confront the character known as Timekeeper and his synthetic army, the Order, in a story that is set six years after the events of Borderlands 3. Be ready to explore a world with diverse environments, battle against enemies, and upgrade your character and weapons. The game supports cross-platform multiplayer, allowing up to four players to team up across different systems, so be excited to play with your friends on any platform! Borderlands 4 is set to release on September 12, 2025, for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC, with a Nintendo Switch 2 version to follow later in the year.

Persona5: The Phantom X

Persona 5: The Phantom X is a story-rich, turn-based RPG that will blend social simulation with dungeon exploration in Tokyo, Japan. The players will assume the role of a new Phantom Thief. They will have to balance daily life activities, like school and building relationships, with nighttime missions in the Metaverse as a Phantom Thief. The game will introduce new characters and features, like rhythm-based mini-games and a PvE mode they call Velvet Trials. However, the game will incorporate gacha elements; nevertheless, it will retain the essence of the Persona franchise. This free-to-play game is set to release on June 26, 2025, for PC and mobile.
